Important Arrival information

This is our BIGGEST event of the year, and when large numbers of customers all try to arrive just before showtime, the entry line backs up quickly. If you arrive after 12:00 PM you will likely end up waiting in a long line of cars to enter the airfield (the line often extends all the way back onto route 17).

Festival Notes

  • Ballooning requires light winds and no thermal activity, so most flights happen in the early morning or late evening.
  • Plan to come early or stay after the airshow to watch colorful balloons inflate and lift into the sky.
  • Announcements will be made throughout the day to inform you about balloon events.
  • Tethered and chartered balloon flights are available, but arrangements must be made directly with the balloon pilot.
  • We'll announce any space available for passengers.
  • Balloon rides depend on availability and weather conditions.

Tethered Ride Details
  • Balloons are anchored with 50' lines
  • Balloons rise to the tether's end, stays aloft briefly, then descends to the takeoff point
  • No reservations — first come, first served
  • Tethered balloon rides are cash only
  • Pay at the balloon — exact cash preferred
