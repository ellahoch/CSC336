Fun for the whole family every Sunday!

Come visit us to see amazing flying feats, from wingwalkers to pachute jumpers. We have airshows every Sunday May through October. Check our schedule for special events!

Hours

We are open every Sunday May through October

Airshow ends at approximately 4:00 PM

Directions

Drive In:

The Flying Circus Aerodrome is located 14 miles SE of Warrenton, VA and 22 miles NW of Fredericksburg, VA.

We are just off Route 17, at 5114 Ritchie Road (Route 644) in Bealeton, VA.

Look for the Flying Circus Airport Sign!

Fly In:

Dimensions: 5000 x 100 ft.
